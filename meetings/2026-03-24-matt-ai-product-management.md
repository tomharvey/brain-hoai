## Key themes

### AI tools becoming core PM infrastructure
- PMs increasingly using Cursor/Claude as primary IDE — shift from email/Slack/docs to code-based workflows
- Non-engineers adopting VS Code forks for PM work
- Harry created a Streamlit app from a script
- Challenge: syncing PM shared Cursor content with Notion (need bidirectional sync between Notion and PM OS)

### AI output quality — the last mile problem
- Claude producing verbose, unreadable reports (700 objects on slides, excessive waffle)
- High token usage, low signal-to-noise ratio
- Need AI agents to edit/refine AI-generated content
- Style guides becoming critical — can't rely on verbal communication of standards

### Automation = codifying business logic
- CTO at Electric Car Scheme example: AutoHotkey script automated 90-95% of underwriting — highest ROI project ever
- The value isn't just automation, it's crystallising what we do
- Jaren conducting time/motion study with video recordings of the new platform
- PostHog activity tracking could feed AI analysis of workflows

### Cognitive load and burnout
- Immediate AI responses eliminating thinking time between tasks
- Burnout risk from constant agent spinning on mobile
- Both experienced phone-based AI addiction patterns
