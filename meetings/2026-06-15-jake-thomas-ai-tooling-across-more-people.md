Before the dashboard: Jake never put notes in HubSpot unless going on holiday. Now ~90% of active deals have notes. Measurable team benefit: anyone (e.g. Adam) can pick up a deal with full context.

Time saving: ~5 min/deal × 20 deals = ~2 hrs/week back.

Used on a call with Tom covering 8 risks — worked well for not losing anything across a complex call. Also trialled on a broker call via speakerphone. Works. Suggested as a strong fit for:
