### Telematics as defensive moat
- Telematics alone is not a moat — fleet management tools embed telematics, competitors can buy telematics companies
- The moat is telematics *integrated into every process*: pricing, pipeline, operations
- Energy trading analogy: we didn't make better predictions, we made *different* predictions (5-minute windows vs 2-month forecasts) — same principle applies to how we use telematics differently
- Broker data is the same: everyone has it, but using it in ways others don't is the moat
- Oli's addition: broker portfolio optimisation based on financial performance data — incentivise brokers through their money

### HubSpot data quality

- Cleaning it up looks like a tactical win but unlocks strategic capability
- Part of my longer-term goal to define the data → knowledge → insight layer (target: develop in Q2/Q3, have answer by Q3/Q4)
