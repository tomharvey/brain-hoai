## Key themes

### Tool stack — all connected

### Broker outreach — semi-automated
- AI gathers information and drafts outreach content for broker communications
- Saves **15 min per outreach, ~45 min total on Fridays**
- Alex deliberately keeps the human-in-loop: reviews and edits every message before sending
- Reasoning: the relationship-building value IS the personal touch. Fully automating it defeats the purpose
- Tom's framing (well-received): "the thing you got it to automate — was that really your job? The thing that makes you special still makes you special"

### LinkedIn automation
- Automated LinkedIn connection requests running in background targeting profiles with "insurance broker" in title
- Used to bulk out network without manual effort
- Also using AI for light prospect research: football teams, personal interests — useful context for opening conversations
- Alex: careful about the "creepy barrier" — uses personal intel for empathy and natural conversation starters, not to show off

### Looker intelligence: naughty and nice list
- Looker provides submission volume + conversion rates by broker
- Alex's use: identify which brokers are performing well vs sending lots but converting poorly
- Current investigation: **wholesalers vs retail brokers** — hypothesis that wholesalers submit more but convert less. Looker corroborating this
- Used in conversations with [[adam-smith]]: evidenced advocacy for specific brokers rather than "I like them because they're friendly"
- Also flagging to underwriters: conflict between wanting more submissions (wholesalers) vs knowing they convert poorly

### Open-ended queries
- Alex regularly asks: "tell me the most important information I haven't asked for"
- Hit rate isn't 100% but occasionally surfaces genuinely useful leads to investigate further

- Claude more direct/brutal than ChatGPT — learned preferences through memory rather than formal structure

- Alex wants to involve [[liam]] in marketing for this — it's more of a marketing function than a BDM function at scale
- Also: new broker activation flows to prevent dormancy after first risk (common problem — agency goes live, has one specific risk, then goes cold)

- See: [[AI-064]]

- Sentiment doesn't tell the full story but adds nuance the numbers miss

- Not yet implemented but Alex sees clear value
