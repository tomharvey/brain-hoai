## Description

Eraaz building a case to replace Lever (current ATS) with Ashby. Same price point. Key AI advantages:

- **AI screening**: percentage fit scores against job requirements
- **Built-in AI note-taker**: replaces MetaView dependency
- **Scorecard generator**: built-in version of what Eraaz/Jordi built manually
- **AI reporting**: automatic conversion rate predictions, time-to-hire/fill metrics
- **Better integrations**: LinkedIn, Cord, JuiceBox sourcing tools auto-create profiles

Lever limitations: no AI, abysmal reporting (Adam did it all manually), manual scheduling, no sourcing integrations.

Adam also wanted to bring in Ashby before he left. Strong alignment.
