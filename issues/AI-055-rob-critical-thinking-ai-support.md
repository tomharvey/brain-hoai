## Description

Rob's current pattern: defaults to delegating everything to AI without first thinking critically about what he wants to solve or why. The output tends to be "live to the island" — do everything the AI says. This is the wrong mode for AI-native engineering, which requires architecture thinking first, then delegation.

Rob needs a different support approach from Chris or Ishmael, who can self-direct. He needs:
- Scaffolding for critical/architecture thinking before prompting
- Context files from David's work to give his agent the right patterns
- Possibly a pair-prompting session (per AI-023 from Apr 2026)

Distinct from general AI enablement — this is targeted support for a specific gap.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Design a short framework Rob can use before prompting (architecture-first checklist)
- [ ] Get David's context files (agents.md, rules/) handed to Rob before David's leave finalises
- [ ] Schedule pair-prompt session (Tom + Rob or Jordi + Rob)
- [ ] Check in after 2 weeks: is Rob asking "what am I trying to solve?" before prompting?
