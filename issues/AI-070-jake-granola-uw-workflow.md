# AI-070: Jake — Granola post-call narration workflow for underwriting

## Context

[[jake-wood|Jake]] agreed to try Granola for post-call note capture. He currently leaves shorthand notes after broker calls that become opaque within days (example: "unlikely to win. Another market is better" — context lost by following week).

## Use cases to try

1. **Post-call voice narration** — after a broker call, speak a 2-minute summary into Granola instead of typing shorthand. Captures the "why" not just the outcome.
2. **Billy paternity leave handover** — get in a room with Billy before he leaves, let Granola record. Don't force a formal doc; let the conversation happen naturally. AI extracts the context.
3. **Deal update narration** — end-of-day roundup: talk through the deals you worked on, Granola captures it.

## Success metric

Jake no longer has notes he can't interpret a week later. HubSpot entries have enough context for a colleague to cover a deal.

## Notes

- Jake is already sold on the idea in principle — just needs to try it
- Close-loss reason problem is the concrete starting point: one real example that cost time
- Billy's paternity leave is time-bounded — this is urgent
