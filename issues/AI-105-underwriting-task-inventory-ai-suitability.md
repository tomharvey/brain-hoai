Before deploying AI in underwriting, we need to map the actual task inventory: what do underwriters do hour by hour, and which of those tasks are suitable for AI augmentation vs. not?

## Action

- Interview 2–3 underwriters to map their task inventory
- Tag each task: AI-suitable (pattern recognition, data lookup, summarisation) vs. AI-risky (judgment calls, edge cases, regulatory exposure)
- Use the map to prioritise the first underwriting AI intervention

## Context

Geran's view: skills-based tasks will shift in 6–8 months, but automation-heavy roles are further out. Underwriting sits in skills territory — the opportunity is augmentation, not replacement. But we need the task map first.
