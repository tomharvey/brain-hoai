# AI-061 — Implement lightweight incident management process

## Context

Fergus raised on a Friday call: uptick in incidents over the past 4–5 weeks. When asked "how many incidents have we had in the last four weeks?" the answer required checking multiple scattered threads. This is the process gap.

Historical context: Flock went through due diligence with essentially no incidents — that process was never exercised. Now it needs to be.

## What's needed

A lightweight unified incident tracking process:
- Single place to log and view incidents
- AI-integrated for detection and/or reporting
- Light — not a heavy ITIL-style process

## Acceptance criteria

Jordi can answer "how many incidents in the last 4 weeks?" without thread-hunting.

## Notes

Jordi's framing: "I don't want to create a super heavy process — just something to have in order to get more data and have more visibility." AI should be part of the detection/reporting, not just a lookup tool.
