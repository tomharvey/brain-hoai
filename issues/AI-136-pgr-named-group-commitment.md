The PACE tool (Francesco and Eraaz's full PGR/1:1 system) is too complex to roll out broadly. The plan is to distill to a minimum viable slice — a single employee-facing skill. Before going live, need a named group of people who are genuinely committing to trialling it.

Commitment must be explicit and opt-in with accountability — not passive "do it or don't". The opt-in group should be willing to use it for the duration of a PGR cycle and give feedback.

## Actions

- [ ] Identify a named group of 4–6 people willing to trial the minimum viable PGR skill
- [ ] Get explicit commitment (not just "sounds interesting")
- [ ] Define what participation means (capture 1:1s, use the skill, give feedback after PGR)
