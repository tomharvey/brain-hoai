## Format ideas

- **Ollie Crowe (Slack)**: breakout tables / revolving tables — worked well at last offsite. Domain experts stay on their table (e.g. Jacob on pricing config). Covers disparate topics well.
- Avoid philosophical debate — focus on doing
- Consider follow-on activities that extend beyond the 90 mins (challenge for the week)
- Could pair people for knowledge codification exercises

## Key tensions to navigate

- Disparate skill levels and topics (agent evals vs "how do I use Claude better")
- "I could have done it faster myself" — true for experts in their domain, but AI unlocks cross-domain capability
- Don't want it to feel like a forced workshop — needs energy
