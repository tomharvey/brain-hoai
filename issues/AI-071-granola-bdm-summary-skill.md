## Description

Sophie uses Granola for meeting notes but the default AI summary doesn't match what BDMs need from their customer calls — it's too generic and doesn't extract the right signals (next steps, broker intent, product gaps, follow-up commitments).

Build or configure a custom Granola summary skill / prompt template tailored to BDM customer meetings at Flock. The output should give Sophie (and the wider BDM team) a structured note they can immediately act on and paste into HubSpot.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Document what "a great BDM meeting note" looks like — fields, tone, what gets captured
- [ ] Draft custom Granola prompt that generates this output consistently
- [ ] Test on 2–3 of Sophie's recent meetings
- [ ] Share with Sophie for feedback and iteration
- [ ] If solid, roll out to wider BDM team via lunch and learn or Slack post

## Notes

Sophie is a motivated early adopter. She already uses Granola; the bottleneck is the summary format, not the tool. This is a high-leverage unlock because the BDM team collectively does a high volume of customer calls.
