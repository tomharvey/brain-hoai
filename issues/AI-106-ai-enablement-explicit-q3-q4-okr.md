AI enablement is not yet an explicit OKR for Q3/Q4. Without it, department heads have no structural reason to prioritise AI adoption within their teams — it remains a nice-to-have.

## Action

- Draft proposed AI enablement OKR language for Q3/Q4
- Align with Geran on framing before taking to C-suite
- Define what measurable progress looks like (frequency of use, coverage across departments, skill level proxy)

## Context

Geran agrees this needs to be explicit, not implicit. The current state: AI is "important" company-wide but not in any individual's targets. That's the governance gap the Head of AI role exists to close.
