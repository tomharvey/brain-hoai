## SLIDE 8 — Activity

**Type:** Content — stays on screen for 35 min

**Title:**
FOUR QUESTIONS BEFORE YOU OPEN CO:WORK

**Body:**

*First: is your work task-shaped (the same thing repeats) or relationship-shaped (context that builds over time)? Your answer changes how you scope the project.*

**1. What's your intent?**
"I want to ___" — one sentence. If it has "and", split it.

**2. What context does the AI need?**
Brief a smart new colleague — files, people, process, what good looks like.

**3. What's your version zero?**
The single conversation you'd have first. Not a build.

**4. Is this one intent or three?**
Pick the most useful one this week.

*Person A answers out loud, Person B helps — then swap. Then both open co:work together.*
