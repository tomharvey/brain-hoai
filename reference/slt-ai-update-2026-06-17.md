## Slide 1: AI at Flock — What We Do

### Headline: "A structured programme, already delivering"

- **Individual activation framework** — 6-stage model used to assess every Flocker and design targeted interventions. Already driving measurable progression.
- **Company-wide + team-specific workshops** — running more than weekly across underwriting, BDMs, finance, and company-wide sessions
- **AI-specific hiring criteria** — candidates scored on concrete AI usage before they join
- **Benchmark survey** completed — ready to rerun for progress measurement

### Where we are now

- **Every team has at least one AI champion** — self-started, building tools for their own workflows, pulling peers along
- **Several teams reaching collective activation** — the BDM team is now working in AI multiplayer mode, asking the right questions together and building shared tools. This is the pattern we want to replicate across Flock — and take to Admiral.
- Flockers are building and maintaining their own AI tools across underwriting, finance, distribution, ops, and product — self-owned, without engineering resource
