# Audience Guide: Fleet Customer

Use this file when the presentation audience is a **fleet operator or fleet manager** — i.e., a Flock policyholder or prospect receiving a safety review or pitch.

---

## Who This Is For

Flock's direct customers: operators of commercial vehicle fleets, typically:
- **Courier** — last-mile delivery (Amazon DSPs, DPD franchises), 80–150 stops/day
- **Taxi / PHV** — Uber, Bolt, black cabs, private hire operators
- **Self-Drive Hire** — rental, subscription, car clubs
- **Trades** — plumbers, electricians, contractors

Always adapt to the specific segment. If the segment is known, use the right framing.

---

## What Fleet Customers Care About

1. **Reducing costs** — insurance premium, claims costs, vehicle downtime
2. **Managing drivers** — understanding who their problem drivers are, reducing incidents
3. **Simple, actionable insights** — not a data dump; tell me what to do
4. **Their premium at renewal** — safety improvement = rebate = lower costs next year

---

## Tone

**Operational, practical, data-led, honest.**

- Talk to them like a trusted advisor, not a salesperson
- Lead with their data, not Flock's capabilities
- Be direct: "here's where you have a problem, here's what to do about it"
- Keep it simple — fleet managers are busy, not data scientists

## Key Messages (ensure these land)

**Safety review:**
1. **"Here's exactly where your risks are — and what to do about them."**
2. **"Safety improvement directly reduces your insurance costs."**
3. **"These three actions will have the most impact before next quarter."**

**Pitch:**
1. **"Most fleets are paying too much because they don't manage safety proactively."**
2. **"Flock rewards fleets that improve — up to 10% rebate."**
3. **"We give you the data and the support to actually make it happen."**

---

## Segment-Specific Framing

### Courier
- Message: "Manage drivers, not just vehicles" — daily reports, driver leaderboards
- Key risks: Urban speeding, phone distraction, fatigue during peak periods
- Stat angle: stops per day, driver turnover, urban speeding rate

### Taxi / PHV
- Message: Safety culture change + ROI demonstration
- Key risks: Urban speeding, long-shift fatigue
- Stat angle: hours driven per week, claim frequency vs industry average

### Self-Drive Hire / Car Club
- Message: "Manage vehicles, not drivers" — protect assets, monitor extreme events
- Key risks: Weekend speeding, unfamiliar drivers, EV battery incidents
- Stat angle: incidents per vehicle, high-speed events, damage cost per vehicle

### Trades
- Message: Operational value beyond just safety — utilisation, maintenance alerts
- Key risks: Complacency, vehicle maintenance neglect
- Stat angle: idle time, maintenance flags, claim frequency

---

## Data to Include

**For safety reviews**, ask the user for actual customer data:
- Total claims this year / YoY change
- Urban speeding rate (target <5%)
- Harsh event rate (target <2 per 100 miles)
- Safety score (0–100 scale)
- Top 3–5 risk drivers (optional — depends on customer preference)

**If no real data is available**, use clearly labelled illustrative figures and flag on the slide:
> ⚠️ Add a small footnote: "Illustrative data — replace with [Customer Name]'s actual figures before presenting"

---

## Things to Watch Out For

- ⚠️ NEVER show individual driver names without the customer's explicit approval
- ⚠️ If claims data is not provided, flag slides clearly as illustrative — do not invent specific claim numbers
- ⚠️ Rebate figures: only show actual rebate if confirmed by user — otherwise show "up to 10%"
- Always confirm: is this a safety review (existing customer) or a pitch (prospect)?
